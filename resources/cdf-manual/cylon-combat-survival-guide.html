Cylon Combat Survival Guide

Cylons are machines designed and evolved to kill, and they travel in great numbers. Once the Cylons return and attack the Colonies, the lives of all humanity will be at risk. We all know from the first Cylon War just how dangerous they are, and they have continued to advance and have undoubtedly grown even deadlier since then.

It is vital that CDF members are prepared to survive under this threat. The goal is not to wipe out the Cylons, but simply to survive this initial encounter in order to escape. Our mission is to preserve humanity and to save lives.

The following sections will provide information on how to survive once the Cylons have besieged your colony, as well as an informative field guide to know when attack is necessary.

Follow these protocols, and you will live to fight another day.
