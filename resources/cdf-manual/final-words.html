Final Words

Ever since the first Cylon war abruptly ended, we of the Cylon Defence Force have been building and preparing for what the future may hold. We fear that there is an inevitable Second Cylon War. Through our knowledge and experiences from the First Cylon War, we have learned that being one step ahead is simply not enough.

You have read the information we have prepared in the hope of giving humanity the upper hand in future encounters with the Cylons.

Now is not the time to sit back and wait for what may or may not come.

Now is not the time to put our trust in an unreliable and unstable government.

Now is the time to prepare, strategize, and look out for one another.

Now is the time to protect our brothers and sisters in arms.

Now is the time to uphold our duties as citizens and as humans; to protect the ones we love and the lives that we have built.

We are the Cylon Defense Force and we are here to protect and serve, at all costs.
